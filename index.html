The Executive Presence Coaching Program

About This Program

This presentation highlights our executive coaching program, which aims to develop essential building blocks for senior executive to represent your company with excellence.

Our program consists of four modules that are 3 hours each and 1 live simulation that is 1 hour which is designed to enhance these skills. Participants will learn skills to present with impact, develop charismatic presence, active listening, and provide/receive feedback.

Commercials

1 hour briefing session with the coachee. 4 sessions, 3 hours each. 1 session, 1 hour. Suggested speed: Can be finalized within 2 weeks or 1 month. Budget: 22,000 AED + 5% VAT.

Module 1 - Presenting with Impact

Feel the imediate impact.

Executives Skills

Equips executives with skills and techniques to deliver impactful presentations.

Delivering Presentations

Focuses on delivering presentations, using powerful language, and delivering it confidently.

3 Hours Long

The module is 3 hours long where the executive will receive direct coaching based on areas of growth when presenting in a boardroom or meeting room.

Module 2 - Charisma Code

Based on module 1, module 2 develops critical elements of executive presence that create a charismatic style.

Critical elements

Based on module 1, module 2 develops critical elements of executive presence that create a charismatic style.

What influence looks

Explores what influence looks like and how to achieve it.

Areas

Areas that may be discussed could be non-verbal communication, grooming, structures or influence tactics.

Tactics used

A review will be done highlighting what are the tactics used by the executive that is atypical from what other leaders do?

Module 3 - Active Listening

Feel the imediate impact.

Active listening

Active listening is a fundamental skill that improves communication in personal and professional contexts.

Participants

Participants will learn how to engage fully with the speaker and develop strategies to overcome barriers to active listening.

Heard?

How does the speaker feel heard by the executive and what are structures to use in order to validate active listening?

Module 4 - Giving and Receiving Feedback

Based on module 1, module 2 develops critical elements of executive presence that create a charismatic style.

Receiving feedback

Built on module 3 we move into module 4 which concerns giving and receiving feedback. Effective feedback is essential for personal and professional growth.

Participants

Participants will gain skills to provide constructive, actionable feedback with empathy and receive feedback effectively.

Structures available

What are the structures available to make any crucial confrontation feel like a healthy dialogue?

Conclusion

The liquid gold of this program.

Wraping up

  • Our final live intervention invites the executive to host a meeting that starts with a coffee and moves to the meeting room. It can involve key stakeholders and introduce a key concept. Total duration of 1 hour. This simulation will allow the coachee to utilize all techniques discussed.
  • Our coaching program will equip your executive with skills that will benefit your company.
  • These skills will enhance executive presence, communication, and personal and professional growth.
